‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I start my first focus task?
A: Open the PomoJoy app, click the icon in the system status bar, and tap the start focus button on the focus page to begin your first focus task. You can customize work and break durations in the focus page settings.
Q: Can I customize the focus task duration?
A: Yes! Go to the settings page where you can customize work duration (default 25 minutes), short break duration (default 5 minutes), and long break duration (default 15 minutes).
Q: How can I view my focus statistics?
A: Tap the statistics button on the main interface to view your daily, weekly, and monthly focus time and completed focus tasks.
Q: What should I do if the app crashes or won't start?
A: Please try these steps: 1) Restart the app; 2) Restart your device; 3) Check for app updates; 4) If the problem persists, contact our technical support.
Q: Can I turn off notification reminders?
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Notification Settings where you can customize or disable various notification reminders, including Pomodoro completion alerts and break reminders.

üìñ User Guide

Quick Start

  1. Download & Install: Download PomoJoy from the Mac App Store
  2. Initial Setup: Follow the guided setup when you first open the app
  3. Start Focusing: Click the status bar icon and tap the start focus button on the focus page
  4. Break Reminders: The app will remind you to take breaks when your focus task ends

Core Features

  • Focus Task Timer: Classic 25-minute work + 5-minute break mode
  • Custom Settings: Adjustable work duration, break duration, and long break intervals
  • Focus Statistics: Track daily, weekly, and monthly focus data
  • Task Management: Add and manage your to-do tasks
  • White Noise: Various ambient sounds to help you focus better

Advanced Tips

  • Focus Task Technique: Define specific goals to complete before starting each session
  • Break Suggestions: During short breaks, get up and do simple stretching exercises
  • Long Breaks: Take 15-30 minute long breaks after every 4 focus tasks
  • Data Analysis: Regularly review statistics to understand your focus patterns

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issue Solutions

  • Timer Inaccuracy: Check your device time settings and ensure the timezone is correct
  • No Notifications: Check app notification permissions and system notification settings
  • Data Loss: Check cloud sync settings and try restoring from backup
  • Sound Effects Not Playing: Check device volume and app sound settings
